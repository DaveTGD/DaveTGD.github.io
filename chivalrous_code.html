Chivalrous Code

Role / Services

  • Writer

    • Credits

  • David Nwachukwu

    • Location & year

  • The United Kingdom ©

  • 2023



    • As a copywriter, I had the incredible opportunity to write a book called Chivalrous Code Vol. 1: What It Means to Be a Gentleman in the 21st Century. This project allowed me to demonstrate my versatile array of skills, and I'm excited to share my experiences with you today.


    Writing a book is a challenging but rewarding experience, and Chivalrous Code was no exception. The book delves into the complex and nuanced world of modern gentlemanliness, providing readers with a thorough understanding of what it means to be a gentleman in today's society. The essential values of respect, consideration, and compassion that define gentlemanliness are explored in-depth, with practical tips and advice on how to apply them to your everyday life.


    One of the most important things I learned while writing this book was the importance of being authentic. In a world where people are constantly bombarded with conflicting messages about what it means to be a gentleman, it's crucial to be true to oneself and to one's values. As such, I made sure to emphasize the importance of being true to oneself throughout the book.


    Another important concept that I explored in Chivalrous Code Vol. 1 was the idea of respect, consideration, and compassion. I believe that these values are essential to being a gentleman, and I made sure to provide readers with practical tips and advice on how to apply these values to their everyday lives.


    One of the most significant challenges of writing Chivalrous Code was finding the right tone. The book needed to strike a balance between informative and approachable, and I spent a considerable amount of time experimenting with different writing techniques to find the perfect voice. Ultimately, I settled on a conversational tone that I felt would resonate with readers and help them engage with the material.


    Another crucial aspect of writing Chivalrous Code was research. I wanted to ensure that the book was grounded in scientific research and backed up by data. To accomplish this, I spent countless hours poring over academic journals and scientific reports to find the most up-to-date and relevant information on gentlemanliness.


    Once I had gathered all the necessary research, I began the writing process. I used a variety of writing techniques to make the material accessible and engaging for readers. For example, I utilized storytelling techniques to illustrate key concepts and make them more memorable. I also employed analogies and metaphors to help readers better understand complex ideas.


    Throughout the writing process, I learned a great deal about the art of copywriting. Writing a book requires a different skill set than writing shorter pieces like ads or blog posts. I had to learn how to structure the book effectively, create a compelling narrative arc, and keep readers engaged throughout the entire journey.


    Overall, writing Chivalrous Code Vol. 1 was an incredibly rewarding experience that allowed me to explore a variety of concepts related to manners, ethics, and authenticity. I'm proud to have been a part of this project, and I hope that readers find the book to be a valuable resource in their quest to become better gentlemen. If you're looking to improve your manners, etiquette, and overall behaviour, I highly recommend picking up a copy of Chivalrous Code Vol. 1 today!
