My interest in Programming began in my early childhood with my unquenching desire to become Iron Man. Which lead me to work on numerous programming projects.

From ink to interface, words and code unite to create worlds beyond imagination....

My Hobbies ...


Travelling


Travelling is one of my biggest passions. I love exploring new cultures, trying new foods, and meeting new people. There's something truly magical about immersing yourself in a foreign land and learning about their way of life. From skiing in the Alps to exploring the enchanting streets of Florence, I find inspiration for my work in every corner of the world.


Boating


Boating is another hobby that I find incredibly relaxing. There's nothing quite like being out on the water, feeling the sun on your skin, and the wind in your hair. Whether I'm fishing, cruising, or just enjoying the scenery, I always come back from a day on the boat feeling recharged and ready to take on new challenges.


Chess


Playing chess is a hobby that I've loved since I was a child. I find the game to be both intellectually stimulating and emotionally satisfying. There's a real sense of accomplishment that comes from outwitting your opponent and making strategic moves. Chess has taught me patience, critical thinking, and the importance of planning ahead.


Game Development


Making games is a passion that I've been pursuing for many years. I love the challenge of creating something from scratch and watching it come to life. From designing the characters and environments to programming the game mechanics, there's a lot of hard work that goes into making a game. But the sense of satisfaction that comes from seeing people enjoy something that you've created is unparalleled.


Drawing


Drawing is another creative outlet that I enjoy. Whether it's sketching out ideas for a new game or creating illustrations for a project, I find that drawing helps me to think more creatively and come up with new ideas. It's a skill that I've been honing since childhood and one that I continue to find rewarding.


Robotics


Making robots is a hobby that I've picked up more recently. I find the process of building and programming a robot to be incredibly challenging, but also deeply satisfying. There's something amazing about bringing a machine to life and watching it perform complex tasks. I love exploring new technologies and finding ways to integrate them into my robotic creations.


Horse Riding


Horse riding is a hobby that I find both exhilarating and calming. There's a real sense of freedom that comes from riding a horse, as well as a deep connection with nature. I enjoy the challenge of learning to control and communicate with these magnificent animals, and the feeling of trust that develops between horse and rider.


Skiing


Skiing is a winter hobby that I look forward to every year. There's nothing quite like the rush of adrenaline that comes from racing down a mountain at top speed. Skiing requires both physical and mental endurance, as well as a willingness to take risks and push yourself to the limit. It's a hobby that I find both thrilling and rewarding, and one that I look forward to continuing for many years to come.
