    • Flash Dodge is a solo project that I developed to showcase my skills as a software engineer, copywriter, and graphic designer. The game is a fast-paced, hyper-casual game where the goal is to dodge the wall and the particles you create for as long as possible to increase your score. Players can create a red cube particle by clicking within the box, and then scroll around within the box to avoid particles and the walls.
